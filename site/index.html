The narrow definition

A VPM is a view over evidence, not an oracle.

Models, rules, humans, or sensors produce scores. A layout recipe states how those scores should be normalized and ordered. ZeroModel compiles the result into an artifact whose cells can always be traced back to source rows and metrics.

A router can inspect the artifact. A human can visualize it. A benchmark can measure it. The artifact itself does not decide what the scores mean or what action follows.
